# About A Dev Writes

Welcome to **A Dev Writes**, a technical blog dedicated to high-performance software engineering, observability, and the evolving Java ecosystem.

## Our Mission
In an industry that moves at breakneck speed, our goal is to provide deep-dives into topics that matter for senior engineers: from **Project Loom** and **GraalVM** to the nuances of **Distributed Systems resilience**.

## About the Author
**Laxman Sharma** is a software engineer passionate about building scalable, efficient systems. With a focus on the JVM and modern cloud architecture, he shares insights gained from real-world production environments.
